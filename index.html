About Me



Hi, I'm Michael.

It's a pleasure to meet you. I'm Michael Folger, a Digital Marketing & Video Production Specialist currently transitioning into full-time web developement. Thank you for taking the time to look at my portfolio, and to get to know me a little better. A little bit of info about me - I am 28 years old, and I live in Evanston, IL with my wife, son and 3-legged cat. I enjoy sports, reading, listening to records, and spending time with my friends. Professioanlly, I am someone who loves to collaborate, to work hard, and to get the job done right. I bring with me 4 years of experience in digital marketing and video production and an extensive portfolio of work in that field. I also speak fluent german. I graduated from Eastern Michigan University in 2015 with a b.s. in Communication and a minor in German for International Business. I hope you enjoy taking a look at some of my web development work, which you will find below.
